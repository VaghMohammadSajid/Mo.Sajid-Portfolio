About us

I am a passionate and dedicated software developer with a strong foundation in backend development and a continuous drive to learn and grow in the field of technology. I hold a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) in Information Technology and have gained professional experience working as a Python Django developer at StackerBee Technology, Dwarka, Delhi. My journey into software development began with a deep interest in understanding how applications work behind the scenes, how data is managed efficiently, and how scalable systems are built to solve real-world problems.

During my professional experience, I have worked extensively with Python and the Django framework to develop secure, scalable, and maintainable web applications. My responsibilities included building backend logic, implementing RESTful APIs using Django REST Framework, managing databases, and integrating backend services with frontend applications. I have hands-on experience with PostgreSQL and MySQL, where I focused on schema design, query optimization, and data integrity. I enjoy writing clean, structured code and following best practices to ensure long-term maintainability and performance of applications.

In addition to Django, I have worked with FastAPI for building high-performance APIs and have gained exposure to customizing and extending existing frameworks such as Django Oscar for e-commerce-related projects. These experiences helped me understand how to work with complex codebases, handle real-time issues, and deliver backend solutions aligned with business requirements. Debugging, optimizing performance, and solving edge cases have become an integral part of my development process, strengthening my confidence as a backend developer.

Alongside my core backend expertise, I have a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I am actively learning AI/ML fundamentals, including data preprocessing, model basics, and Python-based libraries, with the goal of integrating intelligent features into backend systems in the future. I believe that combining backend engineering with AI-driven capabilities can create powerful, data-driven applications that add real value to users and businesses.

Beyond technical skills, I value teamwork, communication, and continuous improvement. I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams, adapting to new challenges, and learning from feedback. I am motivated by curiosity and a passion for building impactful software solutions. As I continue to grow in my career, my goal is to enhance my expertise in backend development while expanding into AI and machine learning, contributing to innovative and scalable technology solutions.
